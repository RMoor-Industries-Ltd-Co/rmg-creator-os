Privacy Policy

Application: Social Manager for RMG
Operated by Renaissance Masters Group LC ("RMG"), which manages the brand social media accounts of RMoor Industries Ltd Co. ("RMI," "the Company") on RMI's behalf.
Effective date: June 5, 2026

1. Who we are and the scope of this policy

Social Manager for RMG ("the Application," "we," "us") is an internal, proprietary social media management tool. It is operated by Renaissance Masters Group LC, which manages the brand accounts of RMoor Industries Ltd Co. on the Company's behalf. The Application is used solely by authorized personnel to schedule and publish content to social media accounts the Company owns or controls, including the TikTok account @busy_mf_.

The Application is not offered to, or available to, the general public. This policy explains what information the Application handles, how it is used, and how it is stored and deleted.

2. Information we handle

When an authorized user connects a Company-owned account, the Application accesses and processes the following, strictly to provide its functionality:

  • TikTok account information obtained through TikTok Login Kit: the account's open ID, display name, avatar, and basic profile fields (scopes user.info.basic and user.info.profile).
  • Authorization tokens: the OAuth access token and refresh token issued by TikTok, used to act on the connected account.
  • Content submitted for publishing: the videos, images, captions, and scheduling details that an authorized user prepares for posting (scopes video.publish and video.upload).
  • Creator information returned by the Content Posting API at the time of posting (for example, the account's posting eligibility and settings), used to comply with TikTok's pre-publish requirements.

3. How we use the information

  • To authenticate the connected account and confirm to the user, before posting, which account is connected (by showing its display name and avatar).
  • To publish or schedule the content the user has prepared to the connected, Company-owned account.
  • To present the privacy-level, comment, duet, and stitch options TikTok requires prior to publishing.
  • To maintain a valid connection by refreshing authorization tokens as needed.

We do not use information obtained from TikTok's APIs for advertising profiling, resale, or any purpose other than the functionality described above.

4. Use of TikTok data

Our access to and use of data obtained through TikTok's APIs complies with the TikTok Developer Terms of Service and applicable TikTok policies. We request only the scopes required for the functionality above and use the data only to provide that functionality.

5. Storage and security

Information is stored on infrastructure controlled by the Company. Authorization tokens are stored in encrypted form, and access to the Application and its data is restricted to authorized personnel. We apply reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect against unauthorized access.

6. Sharing

We do not sell or rent any information. Information is used internally by the Company and the brands RMG manages on its behalf. We do not share it with third parties except with infrastructure or hosting providers strictly necessary to operate the Application, or where required by law.

7. Retention

We retain a connected account's information only for as long as the account remains connected to the Application. When an account is disconnected, the associated authorization tokens are revoked and deleted, and related account information is removed from active systems.

8. Your choices, revocation, and deletion

An authorized user or account owner may at any time:

  • Disconnect the account within the Application, which revokes and deletes the stored authorization tokens.
  • Revoke access from TikTok directly via TikTok's Settings → Security & permissions → Manage app permissions.
  • Request deletion of any information the Application holds by contacting us at the address in Section 11. We will delete the requested data promptly and confirm completion.

9. Children

The Application is an internal business tool and is not directed to, or intended for use by, children. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The effective date above will reflect the latest revision, and material changes will be communicated to authorized users.
